Account Pre-Registration & Email Ownership Bypass

Severity: High / Critical

Category: Authentication • Account Security

Summary

The application allows account creation using any email without verification. This enables attackers to register victim emails and permanently lock them out.

Impact

  • Identity hijacking
  • Account lockout
  • Broken authentication
  • Platform trust compromise

Proof of Concept

Attacker registers victim@gmail.com → account created instantly → no verification. Victim cannot register or recover account.

Fix

  • Email verification required
  • Block login before activation
  • Enable password reset
  • Expire unverified accounts
